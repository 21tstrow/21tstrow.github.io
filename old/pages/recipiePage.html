Ingredients

  • - 4 pounds of Medium Shrimp
  • - 2 pounds of either Italian or Andoille Sausage
  • - 4 pounds of Medium Red Potatoes
  • - 8 ears of Corn, halved
  • - 3-4 large Onions, peeled and quartered
  • - 3 large Poblano Peppers, cut into 3rds
  • - 1/2 cup of Old Bay Seasoning

Instructions

  1. 1. Fill a large pot with water, then add Old Bay and bring to a boil.
  2. 2. Add the potatoes, sausage, and onions, and cook for about 15 minutes.
  3. 3. Add the corn and cook 5 minutes.
  4. 4. Add the shrimp and cook 2 to 3 minutes.
